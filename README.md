# tableau-ecommerce-analysis
Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales performance and user behavior trends.
## 📋 Project Description
Business Challenge:
The company needed a comprehensive understanding of user interaction with the e-commerce platform to identify primary revenue drivers and pinpoint bottlenecks in the sales funnel.

What Was Done:
I developed an interactive Tableau dashboard for end-to-end analysis of sales and user behavior. The project covers:

* **Session Dynamics:** Visualization of session-to-order conversion to track user engagement trends.
* **Audience Segmentation:** Comparative analysis of verified vs. non-verified and subscribed vs. unsubscribed users.
* **Channel & Device Performance:** Identification of the most profitable traffic sources (Organic, Paid, Direct) and preferred user devices (Desktop, Mobile).
* **Geographic & Category Breakdown:** In-depth analysis of revenue by product categories, continents, and countries.

Business Impact:
This dashboard empowers stakeholders to make data-driven decisions, optimize marketing spend, and focus resources on the most profitable market segments.
